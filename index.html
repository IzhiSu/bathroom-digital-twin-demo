Bathroom Digital Twin: Elderly Fall Risk Assessment

This interactive demo showcases a digital twin environment designed to analyze and mitigate indoor risks for older adults. By combining a precise building twin with a dynamic human twin, the system simulates complex fall kinematics within confined spaces.

Key Capabilities

  • Human-Building Interaction: Real-time mapping of human posture and fall dynamics against bathroom fixtures.
  • Spatial Risk Analysis: Evaluates potential impact points (e.g., washbasins, bathtubs) to judge environmental safety.
  • Kinematic Tracking: Tracks hip velocity, drop thresholds, and first contact timing to generate automated risk scores.

How to Use

Use the on-screen UI buttons to trigger different motion scenarios. The "Complex Scenario" demonstrates a full sequence from walking to a potential fall, while individual buttons trigger specific motion.
